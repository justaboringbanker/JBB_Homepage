Plan Your Income

Building wealth is only the first step; drawing it down is where things get complicated. The Withdrawal Scenarios tool lets you visualize the impact of taking regular income from your portfolio during real historical market cycles.

How to Use It:

  • Build the Nest Egg: Set your starting amounts and monthly contributions during the Accumulation Phase.
  • Set the Drawdown: Choose the exact date and monthly amount for your withdrawals to begin.
  • Visualize: Hit 'Play Scenario' or adjust the timeline to watch how your portfolio handles the stress of withdrawals over time.
