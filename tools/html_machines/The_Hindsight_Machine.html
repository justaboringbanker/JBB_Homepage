Experience the Market

The Hindsight Machine is more than a calculator; it’s a simulator for your investing psychology. It uses real historical data to recreate the week-by-week reality of a market downturn. It puts you in the driver’s seat during past crises, allowing you to experience exactly what it felt like to watch a portfolio drop—and why staying the course matters.

How to Use It:

  • Isolate History: Use the slider bar at the bottom to zoom in on different time periods and zero in on specific market events.
  • Deep Dive: Hover your cursor anywhere on the timeline to unlock a wealth of week-by-week data, tracking exact portfolio values through the noise.
  • Test Your Strategy: Input your starting amounts, toggle on the historical panic events, and watch how your money would have weathered the storm.
